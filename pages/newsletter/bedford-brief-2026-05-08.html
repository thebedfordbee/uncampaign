One Reminder

Lights out by 11 to save the birds.

Spring migration runs through mid-June, and again August through November. Artificial light at night draws migrating birds into fatal collisions with glass.

Through June 15: turn off nonessential lights from 11 PM – 6 AM, draw shades at night, angle exterior lights downward and away from windows, and shield or dim outdoor lighting. It also helps the insects the birds eat. Learn more →
