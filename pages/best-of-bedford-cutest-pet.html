A Civic Participation Challenge

Democracy. Pets. Bedford.

This is a Best of Bedford community challenge built around a simple idea: civic participation does not always have to start with a public hearing. Sometimes it can start with a dog, a cat, a horse, a lizard, or one deeply photogenic gerbil.

Submit your pet below. Once reviewed, approved pets will be added to the gallery manually. Visitors can browse by category and vote for their favorites. No downvotes. Bedford has enough of that already.

How this was built

HOW WE BUILT THIS WITH AI

This campaign is committed to showing its work. When AI helps build something useful, it should be explained and made visible — not hidden.

HOW WE BUILT THIS WITH AI

This page was built as part of a deliberately low-cost local campaign experiment. Instead of buying attention through ads, mailers, or consultant-built campaign tools, we are using AI-assisted workflows to build participatory civic projects ourselves.

The concept, copy, structure, and implementation were developed with AI assistance, then reviewed and refined by a human before publication. Pet submissions are also reviewed before they appear publicly.

If a local campaign can use modern tools to build a community pet contest at almost no cost, local government should probably be looking for more ways to make civic participation easier, lighter, and more useful too.
