THINKING OUTSIDE THE BOX

Where This Campaign Experiments in Public

Instead of spending money on ads, mailers, or the usual political interruptions, we're building a series of local participation projects that invite Bedford residents to engage with the campaign in more interesting ways. Rather than buying attention, we're trying to earn it by making things that are engaging, useful, or at least memorable enough to start a conversation.

Some may call it a stunt. Don believes local government should be more creative than it usually is. It should look for better ways to involve residents, surface local insights, and make civic life feel less like a chore.

Explore the Series

Best of Bedford, So Far

Each entry in the Best of Bedford series is a different kind of civic participation experiment for Bedford, New York. Some are competitive. Some are informational. All of them are free and built for Bedford residents.

Fastest Typist in Bedford

A town-wide typing challenge to see who types fastest in Bedford, which hamlet leads the leaderboard, and whether Bedford beats the national average.

Cutest Pet in Bedford

Submit your dog, cat, horse, lizard, bird, gerbil, or other beloved creature and help Bedford decide which local pets deserve bragging rights.

Voter Report Card

Look up your own public voter-history record, see your turnout score, and get a reminder for the next local election.
