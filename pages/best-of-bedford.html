Best of Bedford

A civic participation series for Bedford, featuring interactive challenges, resident-powered tools, and small experiments that make local engagement more interesting.

INNOVATING CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

Earning Attention Through Action

Instead of spending money on ads, mailers, or political interruptions, we're building local participation projects Bedford residents can actually use. The goal is simple: earn attention by making things that are engaging, useful, or memorable enough to start a conversation.

Some may call it a stunt. Don sees it differently. Local government should be more creative about involving residents, surfacing local insight, and making civic life feel less like homework.
