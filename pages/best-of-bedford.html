A Civic Participation Series for Bedford

Best of Bedford

Best of Bedford is an ongoing series of interactive community challenges, civic experiments, and local scorecards designed to make participation a little more interesting. Some will be playful. Some will be useful. All of them are part of a simple idea: if local campaigns and local government want attention, they should be creative enough to earn it.

This campaign cannot afford to interrupt people, so it has to build things people might actually want to use.

A Civic Participation Series

Where This Campaign Experiments in Public

Best of Bedford is where this campaign is experimenting in public. Instead of spending money on ads, mailers, or the usual political interruptions, we're building a series of local participation projects that invite Bedford residents to engage with the town in more interesting ways. Some are games. Some are surveys. Some are civic tools. Some may be slightly ridiculous on purpose.

The point is not to pretend this is normal campaigning. It isn't. This campaign is operating on almost no budget, by design. So rather than buying attention, it is trying to earn it by making things that are engaging, useful, or at least memorable enough to start a conversation.

There is also a larger point. Local government should be more creative than it usually is. It should look for better ways to involve residents, surface local insights, and make civic life feel less like a chore. Best of Bedford is one small attempt to prove that can be done.
