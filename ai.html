The AI Transparency Project

AI Is Not Magic. It’s Amplification.

We are not hiding the use of AI in this campaign. We are showing our work.

AI does not replace judgment, taste, preparation, or clear thinking. It rewards them. A vague prompt produces vague output. A thoughtful prompt can turn a useful idea into something clearer, sharper, and easier to share.

Try the Demonstration See the Prompt Anatomy

The point

The tool did not get smarter. The input did.

The interesting part of using AI well is not the AI. It is the thinking that happens before you press send.

  • A calculator does not make someone good at math.
  • Photoshop does not make someone a designer.
  • A spreadsheet does not make someone good at running a town budget.
  • AI does not create judgment. It amplifies the judgment, clarity, and preparation a person already brings to it.

That is also why this page exists. If we are going to use AI openly, residents should be able to see how we use it, and decide for themselves whether the work is honest.

How to use AI better

          
A Good Prompt Is Just Clear Thinking in Writing.

        


        

          

            
Context

            
What situation should the AI understand before it answers?

          

          

            
Audience

            
Who is this for, and what do they already know?

          

          

            
Goal

            
What are you actually trying to accomplish?

          

          

            
Tone

            
What should it sound like — and what should it never sound like?

          

          

            
Constraints

            
What should it avoid, leave out, or stay away from?

          

          

            
Source material

            
What facts, notes, or examples should it use to stay grounded?

          

          

            
Format

            
What should the finished output actually look like?

          

          

            
Judgment

            
What does a good answer need to respect that the AI can’t infer?

          

        

      

    


    
    

      

        

          
Why this is here

          
We Are Showing the Work Because Residents Should Be Able to Judge It.

          

            This campaign is using AI to reduce cost, explain ideas more clearly, build civic tools, and share the process publicly. The point is not to make politics less human. The point is to make participation easier without raising money, hiring consultants, or pretending a local campaign needs to be more complicated than it is.
          

          

            Whether you support Don or not, we hope this page helps make AI feel a little less mysterious and a little more useful.
